How KOLX AI Works

2.1 AI-Powered Promotion System

KOLX AI automates crypto marketing by engaging with communities using intelligent, data-driven marketing strategies:

  • AI creates posts, comments, and interacts with users on X (Twitter), Telegram, and other platforms.
  • Analyzes trending topics and adjusts engagement strategies in real-time.
  • Customizes marketing campaigns based on project needs and community sentiment.
  • Integrated with Agents.land, providing credibility verification and fair exposure for new projects.

2.2 Trust Score System

Every project receives a Trust Score (0-100) based on AI analysis:

  • Project Transparency – Team verification and past projects analysis.
  • Tokenomics & Fairness – Evaluates insider holdings, liquidity locks, and fair launch mechanics.
  • Community Engagement – AI scans social sentiment, real user interactions, and community activity.
  • Security & Audits – Checks for smart contract vulnerabilities and past rug pulls.

Projects with higher Trust Scores receive stronger AI promotion boosts, while risky projects receive warning labels to protect investors.
