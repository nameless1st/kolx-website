What is KOLX AI?

1.1 KOLX AI Overview

KOLX AI is an AI-powered Key Opinion Leader (KOL) designed to revolutionize crypto marketing with fairness, transparency, and automation. Unlike traditional influencers, KOLX AI eliminates human bias, ensures real engagement, and uses an AI-driven Trust Score System to highlight credible projects while filtering out potential scams.

1.2 The Problem in Crypto Marketing

  • Traditional KOLs often charge high fees and dump tokens after promotions.
  • Many influencers promote scam projects without accountability.
  • Small and mid-tier projects struggle to gain fair exposure.
  • Lack of trust and transparency in crypto promotions.

1.3 KOLX AI as a Solution

  • AI-driven marketing ensures automated, unbiased, and data-backed promotions.
  • Trust Score System evaluates projects based on transparency, security, and community engagement.
  • Revenue-sharing staking model allows token holders to earn passive income from marketing fees.
  • Seamless integration with Agents.land to enhance project credibility and investor security.
