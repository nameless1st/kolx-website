5. Community & Guidelines

5.1 Community Rules

To ensure a positive and engaging environment, KOLX AI enforces the following rules:

  • Respect & Professionalism – No hate speech, harassment, or discrimination.
  • Stay On-Topic – Discussions must be relevant to KOLX AI, AI-driven KOLs, and staking.
  • No Scams or False Promotions – Misleading content will result in removal.
  • Constructive Discussions – Share insights and suggestions to improve the platform.
  • Staking & Promotion – Projects must stake KOLX for AI-powered marketing and holders earn rewards.
  • Unstaking Period – A 2-week unstaking period applies to ensure stability.
