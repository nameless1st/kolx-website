What is Staking with KOLX?

Staking with KOLX is a powerful way to participate in our ecosystem, supporting AI-driven promotional campaigns and community governance while earning rewards. Whether you’re a project looking to gain visibility or an investor seeking passive income, KOLX staking offers tailored benefits for two types of stakers:

  • 1️⃣ Customers: Projects that stake KOLX tokens to access AI-driven promotional campaigns across platforms like X, Telegram, TikTok, and YouTube, along with a Trust Score badge for credibility.
  • 2️⃣ Holders: Investors who stake KOLX tokens to earn rewards generated from Customers’ staking contributions, supporting the ecosystem’s stability and growth.

By staking KOLX tokens, you’re contributing to a fair and transparent crypto marketing ecosystem while unlocking exclusive benefits tailored to your role.

How to Stake KOLX Tokens

The staking process with KOLX is straightforward and secure, with specific steps depending on whether you’re a Customer or a Holder. Here’s how it works:

For Customers (Projects):

  • 🔐 Choose Your Staker Type: Select "Customer" on the KOLX staking platform.
  • 💰 Stake KOLX Tokens: Purchase KOLX tokens and stake them for a period of 14, 30, or 90 days to access AI-driven promotional campaigns on X, Telegram, TikTok, and YouTube, plus a Trust Score badge. Pricing and promotional packages will be announced soon.
  • 🌟 Alternative Option: Offer 1% of your project’s token to gain unlimited promotional access, without staking KOLX tokens.
  • ✅ Confirm and Start: Connect a compatible wallet (e.g., Phantom), approve the transaction, and start benefiting from KOLX’s promotional services during your staking period.

For Holders (Investors):

  • 🔐 Choose Your Staker Type: Select "Holder" on the KOLX staking platform.
  • 💰 Stake KOLX Tokens: Purchase KOLX tokens, connect a compatible wallet (e.g., Phantom), and stake your tokens for a period of 30, 90, or 180 days. Longer periods may offer higher rewards.
  • ✅ Confirm and Earn: Approve the transaction to lock your tokens. You’ll start earning rewards, distributed every 3 months, based on the contributions from Customers.
  • ⏰ Unstaking: After your staking period ends, Holders must wait a mandatory 3-week unstaking period before tokens are released, ensuring price stability.

Note: Ensure you have enough KOLX tokens in your wallet and a small amount of SOL for gas fees on the Solana blockchain.

Reward Distribution

Rewards for Holders are generated from the staking contributions of Customers, ensuring a sustainable and fair ecosystem. Here’s how the tokens staked by Customers (or the 1% token contribution from their projects) are distributed:

  • 💰 50% to Holders: Distributed to $KOLX Holders as passive rewards, paid out every 3 months to incentivize long-term participation.
  • 🔥 5% Burned: Burned to reduce the total $KOLX supply, potentially increasing token value over time.
  • 🔁 20% Ecosystem Support: Used to buy and burn $MAX and $ORAI tokens, supporting the broader ecosystem.
  • 👥 10% to KOLX Team: Allocated to the KOLX team to support ongoing development and operations.
  • 🤖 10% to Distilled.AI: Allocated to Distilled.AI for AI technology and infrastructure.
  • 💡 5% for Future Growth: Reserved for future initiatives and ecosystem expansion.

Rewards are distributed to Holders every 3 months, directly to your wallet, in KOLX tokens. The exact amount depends on your staked amount, staking duration, and the total contributions from Customers.

Benefits of Staking KOLX Tokens

Staking KOLX tokens offers unique advantages tailored to both Customers and Holders, fostering a thriving ecosystem:

  • 🌟 Promotional Access (Customers): Gain access to AI-driven promotional campaigns on X, Telegram, TikTok, and YouTube, plus a Trust Score badge, by staking KOLX tokens for 14, 30, or 90 days—or offer 1% of your project’s token for 1 year of unlimited promotions.
  • 🚀 Passive Income (Holders): Earn rewards from Customer staking contributions, with 50% of their staked tokens (or 1% project token contributions) distributed to Holders every 3 months.
  • 🔥 Deflationary Tokenomics: Benefit from token burns (1% of Customer staking contributions), reducing total $KOLX supply and potentially increasing value.
  • 🔄 Ecosystem Support: Contribute to buybacks of $MAX and $ORAI tokens (20% of Customer staking contributions), supporting the broader ecosystem.
  • 📈 Market Stability: The mandatory 3-week unstaking period for Holders helps prevent sudden dumps, ensuring price stability for the entire KOLX community.

Risks and Considerations

While staking with KOLX offers significant benefits, it’s important to understand the potential risks:

  • 🔒 Lock-Up Period: Tokens are locked for the chosen staking duration (14, 30, or 90 days for Customers; 30, 90, or 180 days for Holders) and cannot be withdrawn early.
  • ⏰ Unstaking Period (Holders): Holders must wait a mandatory 3-week unstaking period after the staking duration ends, ensuring price stability but delaying access to tokens.
  • 📉 Market Volatility: The value of KOLX tokens may fluctuate during the staking period.

Always do your own research (DYOR) before staking.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the minimum amount to stake?

The minimum staking amount is 1,000 KOLX tokens to ensure meaningful participation in the ecosystem.

2. Can I unstake my tokens early?

For Customers, No, tokens are locked for the chosen period, after that the tokens gows to the KOLX Vault.

For Holders, there is also a mandatory 3-week unstaking period after the staking duration ends, ensuring price stability.

3. How often are rewards distributed to Holders?

Rewards are distributed to Holders every 3 months, directly to your wallet, in KOLX tokens.

4. What happens if I don’t claim my rewards?

Rewards are automatically distributed to your wallet every 3 months, so there’s no need to claim them manually.
